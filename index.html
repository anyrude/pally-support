Frequently asked

How do I play?
Pally is a pass-the-phone party game. Pick a game — Truth or Dare, Never Have I Ever, Taboo, Charades, or Kings — add your players, and pass the phone around the group. No account or login needed.

Why does it ask for microphone access?
The microphone is optional. It's only used so you can speak player names instead of typing them. Speech is processed by Apple's on-device/standard speech recognition; Pally does not record, store, or upload your audio.

Do I need an internet connection?
No. Pally works fully offline.

Is there any cost inside the app?
No. Pally is a one-time paid download and every game and pack is included — there are no in-app purchases or subscriptions.
